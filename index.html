I am a PhD Candidate at the Soft Robotics Lab, under the supervision of Robert Katzschmann, Pulkit Agrawal and Oier Mees, working on imitation learning for dexterous manipulation, with a focus on shared representations that enable cross-embodiment policy transfer between human and robotic hands. My research spans vision–language–action training, structured manipulation representations, and large-scale model pretraining, grounded in extensive hands-on experience with real robotic systems and modern policy architectures. With a background in mechanical engineering, 3D computer vision, and robot learning, I take a holistic approach to embodied AI that bridges hardware, perception, and learning.
